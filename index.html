About EcoRide

EcoRide is a mobile app designed for eco-conscious individuals who want to reduce their carbon footprint by sharing rides. Our technology helps match people with similar journeys, enabling easier and more sustainable carpooling.

Key Features

Journey Matching

Users log their usual routes and get matched with others going the same way.

Carbon Footprint Tracker

Track how much CO2 you're saving by carpooling with others.

EcoPoints Rewards

Earn points for every shared ride, redeemable for eco-friendly goods or charity donations.
